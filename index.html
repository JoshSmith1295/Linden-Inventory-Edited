About:

  • This is Inventory System Modified for SERVER NAME

    • Keybinds:

    • Shift + Click: Moves half of the stack to opposite inventory.
    • Ctrl + Click: Moves amount inputted in AMOUNT to opposite inventory.
    • Left Click: Normal Drag (configurable to be like Right Click in settings)
    • Right Click: No Hold to Drag
    • Middle Click: Uses Item
    • Scroll Wheel: While hovering over AMOUNT will increase/decrease value

    Shops:

  • Hold L+Alt to pop up a eye and look at the person behind the counter, left click andon click on open shop,Put in the amount you want to buy in the AMOUNT field and then click the item into your inventory.

    • Item Durability:

  • Almost all items will decay over time (including weapons) The bar under each item will slowly decrease in size as it
  • decays more and more, then eventually fade to a reddish color. Once an item is fully destroyed, it will have a solid red bar.
